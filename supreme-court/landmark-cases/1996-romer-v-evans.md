# Romer v. Evans

**Citation:** 517 U.S. 620
**Year Decided:** 1996
**Constitutional Provisions:** Amendment 14 (Equal Protection Clause)

## Ruling

State constitutional amendments that prohibit protection of gay people from discrimination violate the Equal Protection Clause.

## Constitutional Significance

First major Supreme Court victory for LGBTQ rights. Struck down Colorado's Amendment 2, which had prohibited any governmental body from protecting gay people from discrimination. Justice Kennedy's majority opinion held it 'impossible to credit' any rational basis other than animus. Became the foundation for Lawrence v. Texas (2003) and Obergefell v. Hodges (2015).
