# Gibbons v. Ogden

**Citation:** 22 U.S. (9 Wheat.) 1
**Year Decided:** 1824
**Constitutional Provisions:** Article I Section 8 (Commerce Clause)

## Ruling

Federal government has broad power to regulate interstate commerce; state laws conflicting with federal commerce regulation are preempted.

## Constitutional Significance

Established an expansive interpretation of the Commerce Clause that became the constitutional foundation for most federal economic regulation, from the New Deal to the Civil Rights Act.
