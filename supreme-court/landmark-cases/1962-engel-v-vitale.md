# Engel v. Vitale

**Citation:** 370 U.S. 421
**Year Decided:** 1962
**Constitutional Provisions:** Amendment 1 (Establishment Clause)

## Ruling

State-sponsored prayer in public schools violates the Establishment Clause of the First Amendment.

## Constitutional Significance

One of the most controversial Warren Court decisions. Prayer in public schools became a flashpoint in the culture wars that continue to this day. Established that government-sponsored religious activity in public schools violates the Establishment Clause, regardless of how benign the prayer.
