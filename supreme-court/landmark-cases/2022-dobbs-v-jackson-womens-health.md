# Dobbs v. Jackson Women's Health Organization

**Citation:** 597 U.S. 215
**Year Decided:** 2022
**Constitutional Provisions:** Amendment 14 (Due Process — rejected as basis for abortion right)

## Ruling

The Constitution does not guarantee a right to abortion; Roe v. Wade and Planned Parenthood v. Casey are overruled; states may regulate or ban abortion.

## Constitutional Significance

Overruled Roe v. Wade after 49 years. Justice Alito's majority held that Roe was 'egregiously wrong from the start.' Justice Breyer, Sotomayor, and Kagan dissented: 'With sorrow—for this Court, but more, for the many millions of American women who have today lost a fundamental constitutional protection.' The decision was unprecedented in revoking a previously established constitutional right. Leak of the draft opinion weeks before was itself unprecedented.
