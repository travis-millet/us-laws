# Buck v. Bell

**Citation:** 274 U.S. 200
**Year Decided:** 1927
**Constitutional Provisions:** Amendment 14 (due process — badly misapplied)

## Ruling

States can compel sterilization of individuals deemed mentally unfit.

## Constitutional Significance

One of the Supreme Court's most shameful decisions, never formally overruled. Justice Holmes wrote: 'Three generations of imbeciles are enough.' Provided constitutional cover for eugenics programs. Over 65,000 Americans were forcibly sterilized under state programs. The Nazis cited this decision at Nuremberg.
