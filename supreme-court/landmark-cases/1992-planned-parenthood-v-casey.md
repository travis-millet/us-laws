# Planned Parenthood of Southeastern Pennsylvania v. Casey

**Citation:** 505 U.S. 833
**Year Decided:** 1992
**Constitutional Provisions:** Amendment 14 (Due Process — substantive due process)

## Ruling

Roe v. Wade's essential holding is reaffirmed, but the trimester framework is replaced with an 'undue burden' standard.

## Constitutional Significance

A three-justice plurality (O'Connor, Kennedy, Souter) jointly authored an unusual opinion upholding the core of Roe while modifying its framework. The 'undue burden' standard — whether a regulation has the purpose or effect of placing a substantial obstacle in the path of a woman seeking an abortion — governed for 30 years. Overruled along with Roe by Dobbs (2022).
