# Gitlow v. New York

**Citation:** 268 U.S. 652
**Year Decided:** 1925
**Constitutional Provisions:** Amendment 1; Amendment 14 (incorporation doctrine)

## Ruling

The First Amendment's free speech protections apply to state governments through the 14th Amendment's Due Process Clause.

## Constitutional Significance

First major incorporation case — established the doctrine that Bill of Rights protections apply to states through the 14th Amendment. This 'selective incorporation' doctrine has been extended case by case to incorporate most of the Bill of Rights against state action.
