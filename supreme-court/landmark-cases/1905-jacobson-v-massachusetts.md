# Jacobson v. Massachusetts

**Citation:** 197 U.S. 11
**Year Decided:** 1905
**Constitutional Provisions:** Amendment 14 (due process); police powers

## Ruling

States can compel vaccinations to protect public health; individual liberty is not absolute when it conflicts with the common good.

## Constitutional Significance

Established the constitutional framework for public health police powers. Repeatedly cited during COVID-19 pandemic litigation. Also, unfortunately, cited in Buck v. Bell (1927) to justify forced sterilization.
