# Lochner v. New York (FULL)

**Citation:** 198 U.S. 45
**Year Decided:** 1905
**Constitutional Provisions:** Amendment 14

## Ruling

A state law limiting bakery workers to 10-hour days violated the 'liberty of contract' protected by the 14th Amendment.
