# District of Columbia v. Heller

**Citation:** 554 U.S. 570
**Year Decided:** 2008
**Constitutional Provisions:** Amendment 2 (Right to Bear Arms)

## Ruling

The Second Amendment protects an individual's right to possess firearms for traditionally lawful purposes, including self-defense within the home.

## Constitutional Significance

The most significant Second Amendment ruling in American history. Settled a century-long debate: the Second Amendment protects individual rights, not just militia service. Justice Scalia's originalist majority opinion; Justice Stevens's dissent is equally passionate. The decision left open many gun regulation questions that have been litigated ever since.
